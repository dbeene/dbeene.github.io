I'm a Ph.D. candidate at the University of New Mexico in the Department of Geography & Environmental Studies. Under the guidance of my advisor, Dr. Yan Lin, I study the ways that GIScience and geospatial data converge with transdisciplinary efforts to understand and address cross-cutting health concerns in the southwestern US and worldwide. I advocate for the critical evalutaion of geospaial data as a fundamental part of the data lifecycle, and, using mixed-methods approaches, I specifically focus on the potential for environmental justice and decoloniality to inform and transform geospatial data science.
